Main uses

Auracast 404 is a high performance super plasticizer suitable for precast concrete industry to obtain high early strength and moderate workability retentions are required.

  • Moderate workability retention concrete.
  • Suitable for Self-compacting concrete.
  • High performance concrete.
  • High early strength.
