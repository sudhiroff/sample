About Buildrock

Geniuses pave their way, so did we. Buildrock.in is a start-up manufacturing firm producing a variety of building construction materials. Our firm is growing at a fast pace with the efforts of a dexterous, innovative and experienced team. Our novelty with a blend of experience is our strength. We are small wonders.

We are capable of handling small to large scale projects with equal ease and proficiency. We back our customers to build rock-solid and safe structures by providing them premium quality building materials. We serve customized solutions to our customers as we understand that every customer has a different need. Our R&D department strives to introduce innovative technologies for the benefit of our customers in terms of the quality and the price of the building materials. Our target completion success rate, on-time safe delivery, and other services are unbeatable.

We understand our social responsibility and our working practices are environment-friendly. Our firm is ISO 9001 certified. We believe in value creation for our customers, end-user, and society and thus give them the value of every coin spent by them by offering them sound and distinguished building materials and allied products.
